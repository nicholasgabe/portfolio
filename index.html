About Me


Nicholas Gabella

B.A. in Digital Media - Web and Interactive Media


I'm a recent graduate from UCF with a degree in Web and Interactive Media. I'm passionate about the relationship between the client interface and creating a good experience for the user as they navigate through it. I have experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as graphic design tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Canva, and prototyping tools like Figma. I love to design things, travel around the world, spend time with friends and family, and play video games. One of my life goals is to travel to every continent and experience as many cultures as I can. This will also help with my career choices because knowing different cultures will help with having an understanding of the different perceptions various countries have on certain things, creating good user experiences for everyone, and having proper representation in designs.
