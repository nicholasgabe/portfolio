My Work

Not Enough Salt

The Not Enough Salt project was a group effort to create a cooking website that would simplify and streamline online recipes. We took inspiration from the idea of “grandma's cooking” and we wanted to bring that to life with the design. To view the full design check it out on Figma, or visit the live site!

Study Quick

Study Quick is a mobile flashcard app that lets users create, study, and share custom sets. The design is intentionally neutral in color to help users focus entirely on their study content. It's inspired by the feel of taking notes—featuring a handwritten font and a pencil-on-paper color scheme. To view the full design, check it out on Figma!

FitNik

FitNik is a mobile workout app designed to help users track their exercises with ease. It offers a variety of workouts across different categories, including cardio, flexibility, and strength. Users can choose between repetition-based or duration-based exercises depending on their fitness goals. The app features a vibrant, energetic design that motivates users to stay active. To view the full design, check it out on Figma!

React Native Projects


During my time at UCF, I worked on several labs and projects that helped me better understand React and React Native. These experiences taught me how to build components, manage state, and design reusable interfaces. Working in a new programming language also improved my debugging and troubleshooting skills, helping me fix issues more quickly and write more reliable code.
