About Me


Nicholas Gabella

Digital Media Student at the University of Central Florida


I am currently a Senior at UCF. When I finish my studies, I plan to work in UI/UX design. I'm passionate about the relationship between the client interface and creating a good experience for the user as they navigate through it. I have experience in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, SASS, React/React Native, and JavaScript. I hope to continue refining my skills and learning more before I graduate in the Spring of 2025. I love to design things, travel around the world, spend time with friends and family, and play video games. One of my life goals is to travel to every continent and experience as many cultures as I can. This will also help with my career choices because knowing different cultures will help with having an understanding of the different perceptions various countries have on certain things, creating good user experiences for everyone, and having proper representation in designs.


React Native Projects


Throughout the semester, I worked on various labs and projects that gave me a better understanding of React and React Native. Additionally, working in a new language increased my debugging and troubleshooting skills. When making these applications, I had several issues with inconsistencies in my component naming. As a result, my applications wouldn't render any content. I realized I kept having the same issue so I knew how to fix it easily.
